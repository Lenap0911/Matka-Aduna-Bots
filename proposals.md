# Competition Proposals - Matka Aduna Bots

## Track 1: Prediction and Forecasting Methodology

**Title: Ensemble Modeling with Cyclical Pattern Recognition**

Our GDP forecasting combines linear, polynomial, and moving average models into an ensemble prediction. We analyze 100-year historical data to identify farming and fishing cycles (7-year and 3-year patterns respectively). By calculating income changes during good/bad transitions and incorporating profession-specific earnings data, we adjust predictions based on expected weather patterns. The ensemble approach reduces individual model bias while capturing both long-term trends and cyclical variations for years 101-105.
